About Us

It is more important than ever for college students and new-comers to the PG County area to be aware of what restaurants and food places are around them. More importantly, it is essential to be aware of the sanitary conditions of the places individuals are eating at. Due to the recent salmonella cases surrounding The University of Maryland, many individuals in the area may question where to go eat without risking potential infections or contamination issues. SafeEats is dedicated to finding local restaurants, fast food chains, convenience stores, and more to deliver information on sanitary grades of each establishment to help our users decide the best place to eat at or shop from. Users will be able to narrow down the establishment results by filtering their search by location and sanitary grade. With the results, users are then able to pick and choose from which places have the top sanitary grades and which restaurants they should stay away from.
